Game of Thrones

Set in a medieval-like world filled with political intrigue, power struggles, and supernatural elements, the series follows noble houses vying for the Iron Throne and control over the Seven Kingdoms. With its morally ambiguous characters, unexpected plot twists, and complex relationships, "Game of Thrones" became a cultural phenomenon, blending elements of fantasy, drama, and political thriller.
